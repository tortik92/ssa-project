Product

What is our product?

Our product consists of special soft pads equipped with sensors and speakers. These pads can communicate with each other and are connected via Wi-Fi. Using a smartphone app, users can select games and adjust settings. The pads produce sounds that help users determine their position and navigate the space, allowing blind and visually impaired individuals to experience sports and fitness in a new, accessible way.



How does our product work?

The pads communicate with each other via Wi-Fi. When a game is selected through the app, the pads activate and emit sounds. These sounds indicate the position of the pads to the players. This allows visually impaired and blind individuals to locate the pads and actively participate in the game.



How do you play with our mats?

One example of a game is Memory. There are three mats. First, the sound of each mat is played several times so the player can memorize which sound corresponds to which mat. Then, multiple sounds are played, and the player must step on the mats in the correct order based on the sounds they previously heard.

App

What does our app look like?

Our app is accessible and easy to use. First, you register with a code. Then, you select a game and configure it according to your preferences. Only then do you start the game. Clear labels, alternative text, and logical navigation make the app usable with screen readers.

Do it yourself

What is DIY?

With our own programming language, you have the opportunity to create your own games. This allows you to design and control your gameplay with simple text commands.

How do I create a game myself?

You can start by opening the "Create New Game" section in the app. There, you can describe your game using text. Each decision and gameplay event is defined with simple commands. To control actions and reactions, you enter clear commands that the app will recognize. Before publishing your game, you can test it to ensure everything works as expected.
