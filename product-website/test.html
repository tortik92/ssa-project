Problem statement

The Problem

Visually impaired people are partially excluded from classic sports and fitness activities. There are enough limitations which are not yet solved. Health is wealth: They need physical activities to further improve overall health

The Solution

Creation of digital sports equipment to provide access to sports. Developing of easily accessible usage options. Using the concepts of Open-Source and DIY
